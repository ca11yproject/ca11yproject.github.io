CA11y People

The core CA11y team is an interdisciplinary mix of human-computer interaction language researchers. The project is based at King's College London's Department of Informatics and the Department of Language and Communication Science at City, University of London. We are fortunate to have a diverse team of advisors from a range of backgrounds and institutions, who provide feedback on the project throughout its duration.
